About Me

I am a web developer, and I also have experience with Adobe Creative Suite. I was born in the Dominican Republic, a small island in the Caribbean.

I enjoy listening to podcasts and talking with people. My favorite book is called "The Things They Carried"; it's a fiction tale based on true stories about the soldiers in the Vietnam war. I am an avid Youtube and Netflix consumer.
